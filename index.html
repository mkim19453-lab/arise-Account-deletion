Account and Data Deletion Request

At Arise Habit Tracker, we value your privacy. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

How to request deletion:

Please send an email to mkim19453@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request".

In the email, please include the email address used for your Arise Habit Tracker account.

What data will be deleted?

  • Your profile information (Email, Name)
  • All habit tracking history and logs
  • Any personalized settings within the app

Once requested, your data will be permanently deleted from our servers within 7 business days.
